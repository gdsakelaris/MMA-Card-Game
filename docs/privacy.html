MMA Warriors is a browser-based game that runs entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or gameplay data to our servers. All game state and progress exists only in your browser session and is not saved or tracked.

We do not directly collect or use any personal information. The only data collection on our site is performed by Google AdSense for the purpose of:

Since MMA Warriors runs entirely in your browser and does not collect personal data, we do not store any user information on our servers. All game data exists temporarily in your browser session and is cleared when you close the browser or refresh the page.
